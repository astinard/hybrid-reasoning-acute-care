## Research Pattern

Each document follows this structure:
1. ArXiv search with targeted queries
2. Download and read top 10-20 papers
3. Synthesize into structured markdown:
   - Executive summary
   - Key papers with IDs
   - Performance metrics (AUROC, F1, etc.)
   - Architectural details
   - Clinical applications
   - Research gaps
   - Relevance to concept note
