# Orlando Health AI and Technology Initiatives

**Research Date:** November 30, 2025
**Focus Areas:** AI Projects, Technology Stack, Partnership Opportunities

---

## Executive Summary

Orlando Health is a nonprofit health system with 18 hospitals and emergency rooms serving Central Florida. The organization has emerged as a leader in healthcare innovation, achieving top-tier recognition from multiple healthcare technology associations and implementing cutting-edge AI solutions across surgical, diagnostic, and patient care domains.

**Key Achievements:**
- Level 10 ranking (perfect score) from CHIME in acute and ambulatory services
- Epic Gold Stars Award Level 10 (top 4% of 600+ healthcare organizations)
- 28% improvement in physician documentation accuracy
- 86% patient engagement rate
- Over 3,000 UCF alumni employed

## Current AI Projects and Initiatives

### 1. AIMS System (Artificial Intelligence for Medical Surgery)

**Launch Date:** May 13, 2025
**Collaborators:** Orlando Health surgeons, UCF engineering and computer science students

**Overview:**
The AIMS system represents a collaborative innovation between Orlando Health and UCF to enhance surgical efficiency and sustainability through real-time AI tracking.

**Technology:**
- Camera feed linked to operating room computer
- AI software tracks surgical staple usage in real-time
- Potential for expansion to track additional surgical items
- Improves efficiency and sustainability

**Key Personnel:**
- Dr. Alexis Sanchez, Director of Robotic Surgery at Orlando Health
- Laura Brattain, UCF Biomedical Engineer

**Source:** [Orlando Health & UCF Team Up for AI Surgery Technology](https://www.orlandohealth.com/content-hub/orlando-health-doctors-and-ucf-students-team-up-to-invent-ai-technology-for-surgeries)

### 2. AI for Early Pancreatic Cancer Detection

**Lead:** Dr. Shyam Varadarajulu, Gastroenterologist and President of Orlando Health Digestive Health Institute

**Overview:**
Pioneering AI endoscopic ultrasound technology to detect pancreatic cancer earlier by identifying tiny tumors in the pancreas.

**Features:**
- Advanced AI imaging for early diagnosis
- Clinical study collaboration with European experts
- Part of expanded Digestive Health Institute capabilities
- Focus on pancreatic cancer and inflammatory bowel disease (IBD)

**Source:** [Orlando Health Bolsters AI Technology with Digestive Health Institute Expansion](https://www.orlandohealth.com/content-hub/orlando-health-bolsters-ai-technology-with-expansion-of-digestive-health-institute)

### 3. Virtual Nursing AI Platform

**Technology Partner:** Andor Health and Microsoft
**Platform:** ThinkAndor's Virtual Nursing Module

**Capabilities:**
- Generative and transformative AI models
- Automates over 40% of nursing workflows
- Automated triages
- Real-time expert assistance
- Tailored patient care and intervention opportunities
- Empowers nurses to focus on personalized care

**Impact:**
Enhances efficiency while ensuring high-quality care delivery through workflow automation.

**Source:** [Orlando Health Revolutionizes Patient Care with AI-Driven Virtual Nursing](https://andorhealth.com/orlando-health-revolutionizes-patient-care-with-ai-driven-virtual-nursing/)

### 4. AI-Driven Hospital-at-Home Services

**Lead:** Dr. Jamal Hakim, Chief Operating Officer

**Overview:**
Advanced remote patient monitoring capabilities delivering hospital-level care within patients' homes.

**Goal:**
Extend comprehensive acute care services to home settings while maintaining quality and safety standards.

**Source:** [Orlando Health to Launch AI-Driven Hospital-at-Home Services](https://www.healthcareitnews.com/news/orlando-health-launch-ai-driven-hospital-home-services)

### 5. Triton AI for Maternal Health

**Implementation:** Orlando Health Winnie Palmer Hospital for Women & Babies
**Technology:** Triton by Gauss Surgical

**Capabilities:**
- First hospital in Central Florida to implement this technology
- Monitors blood loss in real-time during childbirth
- Uses digital imaging and machine learning on iPad
- Addresses preventable health complications

**Impact:**
Improves maternal safety through early detection of excessive blood loss.

### 6. Data Analytics and Population Health

**Partner:** Innovaccer

**Results:**
- 28% boost in physician documentation accuracy (~$900K saved)
- 86% patient engagement rate
- Nearly 3,000 additional wellness/breast-cancer screenings
- ~$907K in incremental revenue from screenings

**Source:** [Turning Innovation into Impact: How Orlando Health Is Making AI Work for Patients](https://www.productiveedge.com/blog/turning-innovation-into-impact-how-orlando-health-is-making-ai-work-for-patients)

## Technology Stack

### Electronic Health Record (EHR) System

**Platform:** Epic Systems
**Contract Signed:** November 28, 2018
**Implementation:** March 2019 - Early 2021
**Previous System:** Allscripts (14 years)

**Epic Implementation Highlights:**
- Eliminated 25-33% of 1,200+ disparate systems
- Unified all hospitals, physician practices, and outpatient centers
- One comprehensive health record per patient across all care settings
- Systemwide deployment completed in 2021

**Key Features:**
- **Care Everywhere:** Allows data sharing with other Epic facilities
- **Carequality Integration:** Interoperability framework for cross-EHR data sharing
- **MyChart:** Patient portal for test results, medications, appointments, billing
- **Healthy Planet:** Population health management for high-risk patient intervention
- **Rover App:** Mobile Epic app for nurses (220 iPhone 13s deployed)

**Recognition:**
- Epic Gold Stars Award Level 10 (top 10% of Epic users worldwide)
- CHIME Level 10 rating for configuration and utilization

**Sources:**
- [Orlando Health's Epic EHR Overhaul](https://www.floridatrend.com/article/29050/orlando-healths-epic-ehr-overhaul/)
- [Orlando Health Expands Epic to 7 Hospitals](https://www.beckershospitalreview.com/healthcare-information-technology/ehrs/orlando-health-expands-epic-to-7-hospitals/)

### Mobile and Digital Health Tools

**Rover App:**

- 200+ nurses equipped with iPhone 13s
- Secure chat capabilities
- Medication documentation
- Lab orders on-the-go
