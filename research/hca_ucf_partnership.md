# HCA Healthcare / UCF Partnership

**Research Date:** December 1, 2025
**Focus Areas:** HCA Healthcare Partnership, UCF Lake Nona Medical Center, GME Consortium

---

## Executive Summary

The University of Central Florida has a strategic partnership with HCA Healthcare, the nation's largest for-profit hospital operator. This partnership includes the UCF Lake Nona Medical Center and the largest Graduate Medical Education (GME) consortium in the United States.

**Key Facts:**
- HCA Healthcare operates 182+ hospitals nationwide
- UCF/HCA GME Consortium is the largest in the nation (1,000+ residents)
- UCF Lake Nona Medical Center is UCF's academic medical center
- Direct pipeline for clinical AI research and deployment

---

## HCA Healthcare Overview

### Company Profile
- **Headquarters:** Nashville, Tennessee
- **Hospitals:** 182+ across 20 states and the United Kingdom
- **Revenue:** $60+ billion annually

### Technology and Data Infrastructure
- Massive EHR data repository across 182+ hospitals
- Centralized data analytics capabilities

- Research partnerships with academic institutions

## UCF/HCA GME Consortium

### Overview
The UCF/HCA Graduate Medical Education Consortium is the largest GME consortium in the United States.

### Statistics
- **Residents/Fellows:** 1,000+
- **Programs:** 80+ residency and fellowship programs
- **Specialties:** All major medical and surgical specialties
- **Hospitals:** Multiple HCA facilities in Central Florida

### Programs Include
- Internal Medicine
- Emergency Medicine
- Surgery (General, Orthopedic, etc.)
- Psychiatry
- Obstetrics/Gynecology
- Pediatrics
- Family Medicine
- And many subspecialty fellowships

### Research Implications
- Large pool of physician trainees for clinical studies
- Diverse patient populations
- Multiple clinical sites for multi-center studies
- Built-in clinical expertise for AI validation

## Conclusion

The UCF/HCA partnership provides unique advantages for clinical AI research that few other institutions can match:

1. **Unprecedented Scale** - Access to data and deployment across 182+ hospitals
2. **Academic Rigor** - UCF's research infrastructure and faculty
3. **Clinical Integration** - Largest GME program ensures clinical expertise
4. **Deployment Pathway** - Clear path from research to real-world impact

This positions UCF as an ideal institution for developing and validating clinical AI systems like hybrid reasoning for acute care.
