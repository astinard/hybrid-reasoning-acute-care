# UCF Faculty Profiles for Clinical AI Collaboration

**Research Compiled:** November 30, 2025
**Purpose:** Identify potential collaborators for hybrid reasoning systems in acute care settings

---

## Executive Summary

UCF has significant AI and healthcare expertise across multiple colleges, with particular strengths in:
- **Computer Vision & Medical Imaging** (CRCV - ranked #8 nationally)
- **AI-Enhanced Surgical Robotics & Medical Ultrasound** (College of Medicine)
- **Health Informatics & Clinical Decision Support** (School of Global Health Management and Informatics)
- **Multi-Agent Systems & Activity Recognition** (Computer Science)

The newly launched **Institute of Artificial Intelligence (IAI)** unites 20+ world-class faculty across disciplines, providing an institutional framework for collaboration.
